CUSTOM REVIEW


Can’t believe I’ve not tried this before even though I’ve been to Bruno’s so many times. It’s going to be my default order here now! The risotto was superbly nice and creamy but the star here is the foie gras. You have to top up $12 for it but it’s seriously worth it. It just melts in your mouth! – x lilypad (image by author)

Flaky puff pastry holding sweet and soft caramelized onion, which they could have been more generous with for the price I'm paying really. The crumbled goat's cheese was wonderfully mild and just so slightly tangy, pairing beautifully with the rich onion jam – Jason Wong (image by author)

Bruno’s will always be our favourite, go-to place for great comfort food and excellent service. I can only recommend everything in the menu because everything’s THAT good. So happy for Bruno having branched out yet again and hope this third branch will gain good traction as the first two. – Carlet Delos Reyes-Farol
